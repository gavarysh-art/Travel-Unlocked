💰 Essential Money Tricks

€2 Gondola (Traghetto)

Public gondola crossing Grand Canal. Same boat, 40x cheaper.

Entry Ticket 2026

€5 advance at cda.ve.it (ONLY legit site). €10 last-minute. Fine €50-300. Hotel guests exempt.

Vaporetto Pass

24h pass €25 vs €9.50 per ride. 3 rides = paid off.

Free Water

Public fountains everywhere. Excellent quality. Don't buy €3 bottles.

⚠️ Scam Warnings

Fake Entry Ticket Sites

ONLY cda.ve.it is official. Many scam sites charge more or steal data.

San Marco Food Traps

2-3x more for worse food. Walk 10 min = better + cheaper.

Fake Murano Glass

Street vendors = Chinese made. Real glass only on Murano island.

Validate Vaporetto!

Yellow machine before boarding. Fine is steep.
